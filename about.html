About

Our Origin

Elektro was founded in 2003 by a group of engineers who wanted to prove that people didn’t need to compromise to drive electric – that electric vehicles can be better, quicker and more fun to drive than gasoline cars. Today, Elektro builds not only all-electric vehicles but also infinitely scalable clean energy generation and storage products. Elektro believes the faster the world stops relying on fossil fuels and moves towards a zero-emission future, the better.

Range

Go Anywhere

With up to 405 miles of estimated range and access to the world’s largest and most powerful fast charging network, you’ll spend less time plugged in and more time on the road.

Safety

High Impact Protection

Our model is built from the ground up as an electric vehicle, with a high-strength architecture and floor-mounted battery pack for incredible occupant protection and low rollover risk. Every new model includes Elektro’s latest active safety features, such as Automatic Emergency Braking, at no extra cost.
