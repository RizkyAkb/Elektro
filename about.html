About

Elektro was founded in 2003 by a group of engineers who wanted to prove that people didn’t need to compromise to drive electric – that electric vehicles can be better, quicker and more fun to drive than gasoline cars. Today, Elektro builds not only all-electric vehicles but also infinitely scalable clean energy generation and storage products. Elektro believes the faster the world stops relying on fossil fuels and moves towards a zero-emission future, the better.
